Abstract

This repository contains the coursework and final project for the Quantum Computing M.Sc. course at Ben Gurion University (2020). The course covers fundamental concepts including Algebra Recap, Qubit Measurement, Multiple Qubits, Quantum Circuits, Quantum Search, Grover's Algorithm, Quantum Convolution, Hadamard Transform, Quantum Fourier Transform, and Shor's Algorithm.

Coursework

  • HW1 - Algebra Recap and Qubit Measurement
  • HW2 - Multiple Qubits, Quantum Circuits, Quantum Search and Grover's Algorithm
  • HW3 - Quantum Convolution, Hadamard Transform, Quantum Fourier Transform and Shor's Algorithm
  • Final Project - Quantum Algorithm for Linear Systems of Equations (HHL)
